Privacy Policy — Breathe Slow

Last updated: 6 April 2026

Overview

Breathe Slow is a simple breathing exercise app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data We Collect

None. Breathe Slow does not collect any information about you. There are no user accounts, no sign-ins, and no data is sent to any server run by Searlesoft.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app does not store any data on your device beyond what is required for the app itself to run.

Advertising

Breathe Slow uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID on Android, or IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address and general location
  • Device type, OS version, and app usage data

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by Searlesoft. You can learn more about how Google uses this information at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → turn off Personalised Ads

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
